This file records venue facts and submission gates. It is not manuscript prose.

## Verified venue state

No official EAAI-27 call has been verified as of 2026-07-17.

The official AAAI-27 main-conference page lists Montréal, February 16–23, 2027, and main-track abstract/paper deadlines on July 21 and July 28, 2026. Those dates are **not** treated as EAAI deadlines.

The latest verified EAAI call is EAAI-26. Its Experience Report and Innovative Practice area required development/use context, collected data, motivation from prior work, novelty, and substantial reflection on what worked, what failed, and why.

Official sources:

## Planning implications

- Continue official EAAI-27 monitoring; do not infer deadlines, page limits, tracks, or formatting from AAAI-27 main-track or EAAI-26.
- Keep the Paper Integrator disabled until the current official call and submission format are verified.
- Preserve implementation, validation, failure, correction, human-review, effort, cost, accessibility, and evaluation evidence now because retrospective reconstruction would weaken an experience report.

## Manuscript-start venue gate

Venue readiness requires an official current call documenting the relevant EAAI area, deadlines, format/page limits, review criteria, policies on AI-assisted writing and artifacts, anonymity requirements, supplementary-material rules, and submission system.
