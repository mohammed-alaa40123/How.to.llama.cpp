# Ranked EAAI rejection risks

_Last reviewed: 2026-07-17_

| Rank | Severity | Risk | Concrete mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Major | No evidence that learners or experts benefit | Approve an evaluation pathway; measure misconception correction and code-tracing performance |
| 2 | Major | Contribution reads as a feature collection | Freeze one coherent sequence and common assessment model; cut optional features first |
| 3 | Major | Multi-agent novelty is anecdotal | Extract longitudinal assignments, failures, corrections, costs and outcomes; run fair baselines |
| 4 | Major | Technical correctness is self-certified | Obtain independent llama.cpp/GGML review with dated rubric and issue resolution |
| 5 | Major | Native/cloud reproducibility is asserted from configuration | Run clean-environment matrices and retain exact commands, timings, outputs and failures |
| 6 | Major | Trace viewer provides visual novelty, not educational value | Compare against static source+text and assess tracing accuracy/time/cognitive load |
| 7 | Major | Browser simulation may be mistaken for native execution | Persistent evidence labels, explicit exclusions, and learner boundary checks |
| 8 | Major | Two-week scope causes shallow incomplete artifacts | Prioritize Lab 0 + GGUF misconception lesson + narrow trace; defer optional AI media |
| 9 | Minor-to-major | Generated media introduces factual, licensing, privacy or cost risk | Deterministic technical authority; optional manual generation; provenance and rejection logs |
| 10 | Minor-to-major | Progress tracking drifts into telemetry or research data | Local-only storage, transactional imports, no identifiers, separate ethics-approved pathway |
| 11 | Minor | Model/fixture licensing becomes ambiguous | Keep synthetic fixture project-authored and inference learner-provided |
| 12 | Minor | Accessibility is specified but not exercised | Keyboard, reduced-motion, transcript/static and generated-site tests with retained evidence |

## Stop conditions

Do not begin manuscript drafting while risks 1-6 remain unresolved. Do not publish generated media that encodes technical claims. Do not recruit participants or collect identifiable/behavioral research data without explicit approval and applicable ethics review.
